About us

At EarthyEssense, we believe in the power of nature to nourish and heal. Founded by Bharath, our mission is to bring the freshest and healthiest produce straight from the earth to your doorstep. Our wide range of organic vegetables, fruits, berries, and plant-based powders are carefully sourced and crafted to promote a healthy lifestyle.

We are committed to sustainable farming practices and work closely with local farmers who share our passion for growing food naturally without harmful chemicals or pesticides. Whether you are looking to boost your diet with nutritious superfoods, or simply want the best quality produce for your family, EarthyEssense is your trusted partner in wellness.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to connect people with wholesome, natural products that enrich their lives. We are passionate about helping you live better by making healthier choices more accessible and convenient.

Founder

Bharath, a 19-year-old entrepreneur, is the driving force behind EarthyEssense. As a second-year engineering student, Bharath combines his academic knowledge with a deep-rooted passion for sustainability and healthy living. At such a young age, he has already made significant strides toward promoting organic and eco-friendly products through his business. EarthyEssense was born from Bharath’s desire to provide people with fresh, natural, and chemical-free produce, sourced responsibly from local farms. With a strong belief in the benefits of nature and a commitment to innovation, Bharath is dedicated to making EarthyEssense a trusted name in the organic food and wellness industry. His entrepreneurial spirit, combined with a vision for a greener and healthier future, drives the mission of EarthyEssense.
